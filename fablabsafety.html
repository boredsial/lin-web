Basic Rule

1) First thing first for boys and girls hair must be neat and secured properly at all times, to prevent hazard for the machine and yourself (e.g hair getting stuck, got burn or tangled)

2) Secondly you are required to wear long pants/jeans when they are using equipment in the Fablab, this prevents flying object from hitting the leg like small equipments or parts.

3) Thirdly wearing proper clothing like non baggy shirt , shirt that is baggy will change casue hazard.

4) Lastly wear covered shoes to prevent objects dropping and striking your leg.
